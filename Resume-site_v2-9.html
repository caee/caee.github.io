I did my very sucessful bachelorâ€™s thesis on Brain-Computer Interfacing - the closest thing I could get to sci-ence-fiction in the real world. Here, we created a modular system for controlling a drone with impulses directly from the brain, and had an astonishing 70% accuracy when giving the drone 3 seperate commands; forward, left and right.

The modularity of the system came from the chain of open-source systems used to create our. Each module were changeable - the transduction (electrodes), filters, machine learning algorithms for training, algorithms for send-ing control impulses to the drone, the radio used to send and the module to recieve, and finally the vehicle. This was due to the signals being strictly controlled throught the system.
