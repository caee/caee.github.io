Homebuilt CNC-router, 2020

During COVID-19, a friend and I have been building a CNC-router based on the Mostly Printed CNC (MPCNC - https://www.v1engineering.com/).

All joints are 3D-printed, connector pipes are aluminium, and a 500W spindle is to be mounted at the connection.

More pictures will be added when the setup is complete.

Much work is still needed before a fully working, modular CNC-router is finished.

Future work: Upgrade belt size from 6mm to 10mm. Sturdier pipe connectors - either steel or concrete filled alu-minium. 3D print head. Cutting board mounting system. Work-table upgrades - vacuum, tool mounts, electronics mounts.
